These are some snapshot examples that explain the basic rgb effects effects avaliable with AdvanceMAME. The major intention of these effects is to simulate the visual aspect of a real Arcade Monitor.

Stretch of 200% without any filter

The following examples are from the BombJack game with the game image stretched by a 200% factor duplicating the pixels. Different RGB effects are applyed.

no RGB effect
RGB triad of 3 pixels
RGB triad of 6 pixels
RGB triad of 16 pixels
RGB scanline 2 horizontal lines
RGB scanline 3 horizontal lines
RGB scanline 2 vertical lines
RGB scanline 3 vertical lines

Stretch of 200% with a low pass filter

The game image is stretched by a 200% factor with a low pass filter. The following examples are from the BombJack game with the game image stretched by a 200% factor with low pass filter. Different RGB effects are applyed.

no RGB effect
RGB triad of 3 pixels
RGB triad of 6 pixels
RGB triad of 16 pixels
RGB scanline 2 horizontal lines
RGB scanline 3 horizontal lines
RGB scanline 2 vertical lines
RGB scanline 3 vertical lines
