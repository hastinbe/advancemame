Supported AdvanceCD Sound Cards

This is the list of the sound cards supported by AdvanceCD.

Please note that not all the OEM cards are listed. If your card has a supported chipset, it's probably supported also if the OEM vendor is not in the list.

Please note that some laptop video cards work only when connected to an external monitor, and not with the LCD screen.
