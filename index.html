AdvanceMAME allows you to play Arcade and PC games with video hardware like TVs, Arcade monitors, PC monitors and LCD screens. It runs in GNU/Linux, Mac OS X, DOS and Windows.

AdvanceMAME is a port of the MAME™ and MESS emulators for Arcade Monitors and TVs but also for normal PC Monitors.
AdvanceMENU is an emulator frontend with sound and animated previews of your games.
AdvanceSCAN is a command line rom manager.
AdvanceCOMP is a collection of recompression utilities for your .ZIP archives, .PNG snapshots, .MNG video clips and .GZ files.

If you need help you can Get Support or go to the Forums.
